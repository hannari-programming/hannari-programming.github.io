Local LLMs have become surprisingly approachable. Tools like Ollama, LM Studio, and llama.cpp make it genuinely realistic to run AI right on your own machine.

This session is all about exploring one question together: **where does local LLM actually shine within the broader landscape of generative AI?**

Local LLMs are the main theme, but "honestly, cloud AI seems better for this" takes are totally welcome too. Less about hardcore engineering, more about ideas, experiments, casual chat, and figuring out where these tools actually fit.
